About Us

Welcome To Our Restaurant

Our Restaurant offers truly authentic Burmese cuisine coupled with stunning myanmar ambience and service. Everyone is sure to find something they will love on our extensive menu. Join us to enjoy the best myanmar food for Breakfast , Lunch , Dinner and Snacks .

“ We know how to make a great tasting Myanmar Food”

Breakfast Section

There is no better breakfast dish than a bowl of mohinga for locals to wake up to each morning. The morning signature dish is rice noodles covered in rich and pungent umami fish broth.

View Items

Lunch Section

Burmese Cuisine is not a dish meal, where variety is key.Try something new and treat your tastebuds to a symphony of flavors. The complexity results in dishes that truly nourish the soul.

View Items

Dessert Section

The best way to finish your meal is with our traditional sweet. Our sweet delights are the perfect finishing touch to a hearty local meal. To fully experience Myanmar is to try our famous sweets and desserts.
